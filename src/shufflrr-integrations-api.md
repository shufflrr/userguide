# Integrations and APIs

Shufflrr connects with other apps and services through the PowerPoint Add-in, Shufflrr AI, Shufflrr Blob content sources, authentication settings, and developer APIs.

## PowerPoint Add-in

The Shufflrr Add-in works with Office 365 PowerPoint. In the Add-in, users can connect:

* OneDrive
* Shufflrr
* SharePoint

## Shufflrr AI in the Add-in

Shufflrr AI in the Add-in can search connected business sources, including:

* OneDrive
* Shufflrr
* SharePoint
* Microsoft Dynamics

## Shufflrr Blob content sources

Shufflrr Blob agents can search connected content sources. Supported source types include:

* SharePoint
* OneDrive
* Shufflrr

Google Drive is mentioned as coming soon.

## Authentication integrations

Authentication options in **Admin > Settings** include:

* Shufflrr authentication
* Salesforce integration
* SAML Single Sign-On
* OpenID

## APIs

For Shufflrr Cloud, APIs are available from your site at:

`https://yoursitename.shufflrr.com/api`

More information is available at [Shufflrr Developer API](https://shufflrr.com/product/developer-api/).
