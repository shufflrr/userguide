# What is Shufflrr Blob?

Shufflrr Blob is an AI-powered presentation workspace that creates presentations from files saved in SharePoint, OneDrive, and Shufflrr Cloud (with Google Drive and your network coming soon).

The core idea is simple: presentation follows the conversation. Users describe what they need in natural language, and AI agents return relevant slides, images, and other files from connected business content.

You ask the Shufflrr Blob a question (start a chat), and the agents offer relevant slides you can choose to present or use in your PowerPoint deck.

Shufflrr Blob has four main areas:

1. **Workspace** — prompt the Blob and review results  
2. **Content Sources** — connect repositories the agents search  
3. **Agents** — configure AI workers and what they can use  
4. **Analytics** — usage, broadcasts, and activity
